01 — Industrial Applications

Where Lithium Goes

01

Electric Vehicle Batteries

Lithium carbonate and lithium hydroxide are primary inputs for cathode active material in NMC, NCA, and LFP battery chemistries used across passenger and commercial EV platforms.

02

Grid-Scale Energy Storage

Utility-scale battery storage systems rely on high-purity lithium salts to maintain consistent electrochemical performance across thousands of charge-discharge cycles.

03

Consumer Electronics

Laptops, smartphones, and portable devices depend on lithium-ion cells that require consistent purity profiles to meet OEM cycle life and safety standards.

04

Specialty Glass & Ceramics

Lithium compounds improve thermal resistance and reduce thermal expansion coefficients in specialty glass, technical ceramics, and frits for industrial applications.

05

Lubricants & Greases

Lithium-based grease thickeners are the global standard for high-temperature, high-load industrial lubrication in aerospace, automotive, and heavy equipment sectors.

SYNCORE™ Facility
SYNCORE™ Facility — Refining Intelligence

Syntra's SYNCORE™ platform processes lithium-bearing feedstocks — including spodumene concentrates, lithium-ion battery black mass, and cathode manufacturing scrap — through a closed-loop hydrometallurgical flowsheet operating at moderate temperatures and ambient pressure. This modular, feedstock-agnostic architecture eliminates reliance on high-energy pyrometallurgical smelting while delivering battery-grade lithium carbonate and lithium hydroxide at >99% purity. AI-driven process control continuously optimises leaching chemistry, precipitation parameters, and reagent recovery to maintain consistent trace metal profiles across production batches. Industrial-scale deployment across Syntra's distributed refining network enables supply chain resilience, reducing single-point concentration risk while integrating cleanly into North American cathode precursor and cell manufacturing supply chains.
