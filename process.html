The Supply Chain Gap

A Structural Problem
In Global Refining

Critical minerals are mined globally but refining capacity is deeply concentrated. Strategic industries cannot afford fragmented, geographically dispersed supply chains for materials that power the energy transition.

Demand Growth

Global critical mineral demand is projected to triple by 2040, driven by electrification and advanced manufacturing expansion.

Platform Advantage

What Makes
SYNTRA Different

Feedstock-Agnostic Processing

One platform accepts mining concentrates, battery black mass, and manufacturing scrap — no feedstock lock-in.

Modular Refining Infrastructure

Designed to scale from 1,000 TPA pilot to network-scale deployment. Modular architecture enables phased capital deployment.

Closed-Loop Processing Systems

Zero liquid discharge. Reagents are recovered and recycled within the system. Near-zero waste generation and 60–75% lower energy vs smelting.

Digital Process Optimization

AI-driven digital twin models control process chemistry in real time. Predictive models maximize recovery, reduce downtime, and maintain certified output purity.
