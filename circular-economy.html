Process Stages

Mine

Extraction of critical minerals including cobalt, lithium, nickel, manganese, and copper from primary sources.

Processing

Initial treatment and beneficiation of raw materials to remove impurities and prepare for refining.

Refining

Advanced purification to achieve battery-grade or industrial-grade material specifications.

Dual Use Raw Materials

High-quality refined materials suitable for multiple applications across various industries.

Production Scrap

Manufacturing waste and off-spec materials that can be immediately recycled back into the process.

End of Life

Products that have completed their useful life in batteries, electronics, and other applications.

Recycling

Recovery of valuable materials from end-of-life products and production scrap for reintegration.
