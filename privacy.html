Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Syntra Refining Corp. ("Syntra", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information in connection with our website, partner portals, and related services.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, organization, and role.
  • Business information shared through forms, NDAs, or commercial discussions.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and usage analytics.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to commercial, technical, or career inquiries.
  • To provide access to partner or observer portals under agreed terms.
  • To maintain security, monitor abuse, and improve our digital infrastructure.

3. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers who support our IT, security, or communication systems.
  • Professional advisors (legal, financial, or technical) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Regulators or authorities where required by law.

4. Data retention & security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel, and we apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate for our size and risk profile.

5. Your choices

You may request access, updates, or deletion of your personal information where applicable. You can also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@syntrarefining.com.

6. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect the current version.
