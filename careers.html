Hiring Process

Our interview process

We prioritize technical depth and hands-on experience over credentials alone. Our process is designed to be efficient and respectful of your time—most candidates complete the full cycle in 2-3 weeks from first conversation to offer.

01 • ~20 MIN

Initial screening

Brief conversation with our team to discuss your background, technical experience, and what you're looking to build in your next role.

02 • ~60 MIN

Technical deep dive

In-depth discussion of systems you've designed and shipped—process equipment, control systems, production software, or R&D projects.

03 • ~90 MIN

Collaborative session

Work through a real technical problem with our engineering team—design review, process analysis, or system architecture.

04 • ~1 WEEK

Offer & onboarding

Transparent compensation package and structured onboarding plan connected to active production projects.
