Hiring Process

Our interview process

We prioritize technical depth and hands-on experience over credentials alone. Our process is designed to be efficient and respectful of your time—most candidates complete the full cycle in 2-3 weeks from first conversation to offer.

01 • ~20 MIN

Initial screening

A brief call to learn about your background, what drew you to critical minerals refining, and what you want to work on next.

02 • ~60 MIN

Technical deep dive

A focused discussion on your domain expertise—refining chemistry, feedstock handling, process engineering, commercial deal flow, or operations depending on the role.

03 • ~90 MIN

Working session

Engage directly with the team on a real problem—process optimization, feedstock evaluation, a commercial scenario, or an operational challenge relevant to your role.

04 • ~1 WEEK

Offer & onboarding

A clear, transparent offer followed by structured onboarding—you'll be contributing to active refining projects from day one.
