Hiring Process

How we hire

We move quickly and focus on real technical depth over credentials. Most processes take 2-3 weeks from first contact to offer.

01 • ~20 MIN

Intro call

15–20 minute conversation focused on your background and what you want to build.

02 • ~60 MIN

Technical deep dive

Discussion of real projects you've shipped — lab work, control systems, or software.

03 • ~90 MIN

Working session

Collaborative whiteboard or design session with the Syntra team.

04 • 1 WEEK

Offer & onboarding

Clear comp structure and a structured onboarding plan tied to live projects.
