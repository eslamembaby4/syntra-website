Hiring Process

Our interview process

We prioritize technical depth and hands-on experience over credentials alone. Most candidates complete the full cycle in 2–3 weeks from first conversation to offer.

A • ~20 MIN

Initial screening

A brief call to learn about your background, what drew you to critical minerals refining, and what you want to work on next.

B • ~60 MIN

Technical deep dive

A focused discussion on your domain expertise: refining chemistry, feedstock handling, process engineering, commercial deal flow, or operations depending on the role.

C • ~1 WEEK

Offer & onboarding

A clear, transparent offer followed by structured onboarding. You'll be contributing to active refining projects from day one.
