Cobalt — The Stability Metal

Cobalt enhances lithium-ion battery performance — improving energy density, thermal stability, and overall cycle lifespan. It is a foundational input to the battery chemistries powering EVs and stationary storage systems globally.

Canada holds the world's 7th-largest cobalt reserves — yet much of its cobalt is exported raw for overseas processing, then repurchased as battery-grade material at a premium. This structural vulnerability represents both a national security risk and a commercial opportunity.

SYNCORE™ Technology — Cobalt Recovery

Syntra's proprietary SYNCORE™ platform applies advanced hydrometallurgy combined with AI process optimization to recover cobalt without toxic solvents. The result: cleaner processing, stronger economics, and output that meets battery-grade specifications — domestically.
