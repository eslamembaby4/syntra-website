Cobalt —
The Stability Metal

Cobalt enhances lithium-ion battery performance — improving energy density, thermal stability, and cycle lifespan. It is a foundational input to battery chemistries powering EVs and stationary storage systems globally.

Canada holds the world's 7th-largest cobalt reserves — yet most is exported raw, processed overseas, and repurchased as battery-grade material at a premium. This structural vulnerability represents both a national security risk and a commercial opportunity.

Syntra's SYNCORE™ platform applies advanced hydrometallurgy combined with AI process optimization to recover cobalt without toxic solvents — delivering cleaner processing, stronger economics, and battery-grade output domestically.
