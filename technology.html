Zero liquid discharge, near-zero waste, and 60–75% less energy consumption than smelting. SYNCORE™ operates at moderate temperatures and ambient pressure — enabling scalable deployment across North America.

01 — INTAKE

Feedstock Agnostic

Lithium-ion black mass, spodumene concentrates, nickel sulfide concentrates, and cathode manufacturing scrap all flow through the same unit operations — only parameters change.

02 — PROCESS

Solid/Liquid Extraction

Advanced hydrometallurgical process using solid/liquid extraction at moderate temperatures and ambient pressure — dramatically reducing energy intensity versus traditional pyrometallurgy.

03 — OUTPUT

Certified Purity

Li carbonate/hydroxide >99% purity. Ni and Co sulfates to customer specifications. Consistent batch-to-batch quality meeting aerospace, defence, and battery qualification requirements.
