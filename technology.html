Core Technology Platform

Feedstock-Agnostic,
Closed-Loop Chemistry

SynCore™ processes complex, heterogeneous mineral inputs that traditional smelters cannot handle economically. Primary concentrates, intermediate refining products, mixed-metal streams, and end-of-life battery materials all flow through the same hydrometallurgical architecture. Chemistry adjustments are parametric, not structural.

The closed-loop system design eliminates liquid discharge entirely. All process water recirculates continuously. Residual solid streams are stabilized for compliant storage or converted to industrial by-products. Operating temperatures between ambient and 90°C reduce energy consumption by 60-75% compared to pyrometallurgical baselines.

Multi-output purity is engineered through sequential separation stages. Controlled precipitation, solvent extraction, and crystallization each target specific elements, removing impurities systematically to deliver battery-grade specifications across lithium, nickel, cobalt, and manganese product streams simultaneously.

SynCore™ is standardized, modular, and AI-controlled. Every refining node uses identical unit operations, enabling predictable deployment timelines, capital costs, and operational performance regardless of installation location or feedstock source.

Competitive Advantages

Technical Differentiation Through Process Design

Feedstock Agnostic Processing

SynCore is designed to process multiple feedstock categories through a unified process architecture. Primary concentrates, intermediate refining products, and end-of-life battery materials all flow through the same core unit operations, with chemistry adjustments managed via process control parameters rather than infrastructure modifications.

This operational flexibility mitigates supply chain concentration risk, enables dynamic feedstock blending to optimize economics, and positions the platform to capture value from the emerging battery recycling market as post-consumer material volumes increase.

Battery-Grade Purity Assurance

Hydrometallurgical processing delivers precision impurity control that pyrometallurgical routes cannot consistently achieve. Each separation stage—leaching, precipitation, solvent extraction, and crystallization—targets specific contaminant removal, with inline analytical monitoring ensuring specification compliance before material advances to subsequent processing steps.

Product output consistently meets or exceeds original equipment manufacturer specifications for lithium hydroxide monohydrate, nickel sulfate hexahydrate, cobalt sulfate heptahydrate, and manganese sulfate monohydrate. This reliability eliminates the need for additional downstream purification and reduces offtake counterparty qualification timelines.

Closed-Loop Environmental Systems

Zero liquid discharge is engineered into the core process design. All process water is continuously recycled through the refining circuit, with water losses limited to product moisture content and atmospheric evaporation. Residual solid streams are either stabilized for environmentally compliant long-term storage or converted into saleable industrial by-products.

Operating temperatures ranging from ambient to 90°C reduce thermal energy requirements by approximately 60-75% relative to pyrometallurgical baselines. Additional efficiency gains are captured through systematic waste heat recovery and cross-process thermal integration, further reducing the carbon intensity of final products.

Deployment Architecture

Modular Infrastructure for Rapid Capacity Scaling

SynCore is architected as a standardized modular platform designed for incremental capacity expansion. Individual refining modules are engineered for optimal throughput efficiency at 500 to 2,000 tonnes per annum of refined product output, with specific capacity determined by feedstock mineralogy, target product specifications, and recovery rate assumptions.

Capacity growth is achieved through module replication rather than facility redesign. Each additional module follows identical engineering specifications, procurement schedules, and commissioning protocols, substantially compressing project development timelines and reducing per-unit capital expenditure through equipment standardization and volume purchasing agreements.

All refining nodes are connected to a centralized process control and data acquisition system, enabling real-time performance monitoring, predictive maintenance scheduling, and network-wide process optimization. Operational learnings from any individual node are immediately accessible across the entire facility network, creating a continuous improvement feedback loop that enhances system-wide efficiency over time.

The standardized chemistry and equipment configuration across all nodes de-risks expansion capital deployment, accelerates permitting and commissioning timelines, and ensures predictable operating performance as the refining network scales to meet contracted offtake volumes.

Operational Intelligence

AI-Driven Process Control Layer

SynCore™ integrates machine learning models directly into operational control systems. Real-time feedstock composition analysis triggers parametric adjustments across leaching pH, temperature profiles, reagent dosing rates, and residence times—optimizing recovery and purity without manual intervention.

This is not analytics or reporting. This is closed-loop operational control where AI makes continuous process decisions based on live sensor data, historical performance patterns, and target product specifications.

ADAPTIVE CHEMISTRY

Dynamic Parameter Optimization

Feed mineralogy varies batch-to-batch. AI models predict optimal process conditions for each feedstock profile, adjusting chemistry in real-time to maintain consistent output quality despite input variability.

PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE

Equipment Health Monitoring

Sensor arrays track equipment performance metrics. Machine learning identifies degradation patterns before failures occur, enabling scheduled maintenance during planned downtime and maintaining target uptime above 95%.

NETWORK LEARNING

Cross-Facility Optimization

Process improvements identified at any SynHub™ facility are immediately available network-wide. Every refining node benefits from operational learnings across the entire system, creating compounding efficiency gains over time.

QUALIFICATION ASSURANCE

Specification Compliance

AI-controlled quality gates prevent off-spec material from advancing through processing stages. Inline analytics verify impurity levels before crystallization, ensuring every batch meets or exceeds customer specifications.

Traditional refining relies on human operators adjusting process parameters based on periodic sampling and experience. SynCore™ makes those decisions automatically, continuously, and with precision impossible through manual control.

This operational intelligence layer is what enables rapid deployment, consistent performance, and continuous improvement across distributed facilities.
