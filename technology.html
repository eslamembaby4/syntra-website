Core Technology Platform

Feedstock-Agnostic,
Closed-Loop Chemistry

SYNCORE™ processes multiple feedstock types through standardized hydrometallurgical architecture. Zero liquid discharge. Energy consumption reduced 60-75% versus traditional smelting.

Traditional pyrometallurgical processing requires high temperatures, significant energy input, and produces liquid waste streams requiring extensive treatment. SYNCORE™ eliminates these limitations through precision hydrometallurgical chemistry operating at ambient pressure and moderate temperatures.

The platform accepts lithium-ion black mass, spodumene concentrates, nickel sulfide concentrates, and cathode manufacturing scrap. Each feedstock type flows through the same unit operations with parameter adjustments controlled by digital twin models calibrated to material chemistry.

Key Capabilities

Platform Advantages

01

Feedstock Flexibility

Process black mass, concentrates, and manufacturing scrap through identical unit operations. No custom engineering required for feedstock variations. Chemistry parameters adjust automatically based on real-time assay data.

02

Battery-Grade Output

Lithium carbonate/hydroxide >99.5% purity. Nickel and cobalt sulfates to customer specifications. Controlled trace metal profiles meeting OEM qualification requirements. Consistent batch-to-batch quality.

03

Closed-Loop System

Zero liquid discharge through reagent recycling and water recovery systems. Process water circulates continuously. Waste streams minimized to filter cake suitable for safe disposal or further processing.

Operational Intelligence

AI-Driven Process Control

Machine learning models integrated into operational control. Real-time adjustments optimize recovery and purity across the distributed network.

Digital twin architecture creates virtual representations of each refining stage. Sensor data flows continuously into predictive models that calculate optimal reagent concentrations, flow rates, temperature, and pH levels. The system responds to feedstock variability without manual operator intervention.

Data from all nodes feeds centralized machine learning infrastructure. Process improvements validated at one facility deploy automatically across the network. This distributed learning approach accelerates optimization beyond what single-site operations can achieve.
