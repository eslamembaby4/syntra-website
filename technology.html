INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGY

SYNCORE
REDEFINING
MINERAL REFINING

SYNCORE™ is an innovative, feedstock-agnostic, closed-loop technology using solid/liquid extraction to refine critical minerals. AI-driven process control continuously adjusts chemistry to maximize yield, minimize downtime, and maintain battery-grade purity specifications.

CORE TECHNOLOGY PLATFORM

Feedstock-Agnostic,
Closed-Loop Chemistry

Zero liquid discharge, near-zero waste, and 60-75% less energy consumption than smelting.

Traditional pyrometallurgy requires high temperatures, high energy input, and produces liquid waste streams. SYNCORE™ operates at moderate temperatures and ambient pressure.

Platform supports lithium-ion black mass, spodumene concentrates, nickel sulfide concentrates, and cathode manufacturing scrap. Feedstocks flow through the same unit operations with parameter adjustments controlled by digital twin models.

KEY CAPABILITIES

Platform Advantages

01

Battery-Grade Output

Lithium carbonate/hydroxide >99.5% purity. Nickel and cobalt sulfates to customer specifications. Controlled trace metal profiles meeting OEM qualification requirements. Consistent batch-to-batch quality.

02

Closed-Loop System

Zero liquid discharge through reagent recycling and water recovery systems. Process water circulates continuously. Waste streams minimized to filter cake suitable for safe disposal or further processing.

OPERATIONAL INTELLIGENCE

AI-Driven Process Control

Machine learning models integrated into operational control. Real-time adjustments optimize recovery and purity across the distributed network.

Real-Time Processing

Sensor data flows continuously into predictive models that calculate optimal parameters in real-time.

Digital Twin

Virtual representations of each refining stage synchronized with physical operations for predictive control.
