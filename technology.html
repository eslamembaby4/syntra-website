Feedstock Agnostic

Lithium-ion black mass, spodumene concentrates, nickel sulfide concentrates, and cathode manufacturing scrap all flow through the same unit operations. Only process parameters change.

Solid-Liquid Extraction

Advanced hydrometallurgical process using solid-liquid extraction at moderate temperatures, with significantly lower energy intensity than traditional pyrometallurgy.

Purity Specifications

Li carbonate and hydroxide targeting >99% purity. Ni and Co sulfates to customer specifications. Output quality designed to meet aerospace, defence, and battery qualification requirements.
