Feedstock Agnostic

Our advanced hydrometallurgical process handles diverse supply streams across the full mineral cycle:

Mining Stream

We process mine-site concentrates and tailings directly, recovering critical minerals without requiring export or intermediate smelting.

Recycling Stream

End-of-life lithium-ion batteries contain recoverable lithium, cobalt, nickel, and manganese. We process black mass into battery-grade outputs, closing the loop on battery mineral supply.

Manufacturing Stream

Cathode scrap and electrode trimmings from battery manufacturers contain high concentrations of refined minerals. We recover them at source, returning material directly into the supply chain without export.
