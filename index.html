UNIVERSAL INTAKE

Feedstock Agnostic

Enabling the Circular Economy — Our advanced hydrometallurgical process safely and efficiently digests diverse supply streams:

Mining Stream

Our Mining Stream solution refines mine-site concentrates and tailings with high efficiency, recovering critical minerals at scale while reducing waste across the extraction lifecycle.

Recycling Stream

Black Mass intake supports circularity by recovering high-value materials from end-of-life lithium-ion batteries. This pathway strengthens supply-chain resilience, enhances sustainability, and returns critical minerals back into productive use.

Manufacturing Stream

We refine high-value manufacturing scrap to recapture essential materials and reduce waste at the source. This stream reinforces sustainable production, boosts supply-chain efficiency delivering meaningful value back to OEMs.
