05 // REFINING IS THE BOTTLENECK. CAPACITY IS CRITICALLY LOW

Technology Limitations

Environmental impact limit refiners

Large volumes of Toxic solvents used in hydrometallurgical plants, while the high energy use in pyrometallurgy plants contribute GHG emissions.

Ore complexities limits refining capability

Standard refineries require "Perfect Ore." They cannot handle low-grade concentrates or mixed waste, yielding significant waste and high refining costs for upstream producers.

Technology limits Recovery economics

High operating costs, CAPEX, and complex feedstock reduce margins and recovery economics, increasing payback periods and challenging investability.
