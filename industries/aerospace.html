SECTOR ANALYSIS

Industry Challenges

01

Extreme Purity Requirements

Aerospace components demand ultra-high-purity nickel, aluminum, titanium, and cobalt alloys — with trace element specifications far stricter than industrial grade — to ensure material integrity under flight stress and thermal cycling.

02

Qualified Supplier Constraints

Aerospace OEMs operate under strict approved supplier programs with long qualification timelines — limiting the ability to rapidly onboard new sources even as existing supply chains face geopolitical disruption.

03

Conflict Mineral Compliance

Aerospace manufacturers face stringent conflict mineral due diligence requirements — necessitating documented, auditable supply chains from extraction through refining to component production.

04

Western Refining Capacity Gap

The gap between Western mining capacity and Western refining capacity for aerospace-grade materials creates structural supply vulnerabilities that geopolitical events can rapidly convert into production halts.
