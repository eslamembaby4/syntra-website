The Refining Bottleneck

The Missing Link in
North America's Supply Chain

UPSTREAM

Mining Solved

North America has abundant critical mineral deposits. Extraction technology is mature. Mines are being permitted and developed.

MIDSTREAM

Refining Bottleneck

Refining capacity is concentrated offshore. Domestic infrastructure is fragmented, outdated, and insufficient to meet demand. This is the constraint.

DOWNSTREAM

Demand Secured

Battery manufacturers and OEMs are investing billions. Demand for refined materials is guaranteed. Supply is the challenge.

Syntra Refining exists to eliminate this bottleneck. We deploy modular refining systems that process complex feedstocks domestically, delivering battery-grade materials at the scale and speed the market requires.

We are building the systems that make North American supply chains viable.

Our Mission

Deploying Modular
Refining Infrastructure

Monolithic smelters are capital-intensive, inflexible, and environmentally problematic. Syntra deploys modular hydrometallurgical systems that can be rapidly installed, scaled incrementally, and optimized continuously through AI-driven process control.

Repeatable Systems

Our SYNHUB™ platform is a standardized, modular refining system designed for rapid deployment. Each installation follows proven design templates, enabling predictable timelines, cost structures, and performance metrics.

This is not custom engineering. We replicate what works, reducing execution risk and accelerating time to production.

Operational Excellence

Our systems incorporate AI-driven process control that continuously optimizes yield, purity, and efficiency based on real-time data from feedstock analysis and downstream specifications.

Industrial reliability and qualification-ready output are non-negotiable requirements. We deliver both.

Leadership

Executive Leadership

Nifemi Oguntuase

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Nifemi Oguntuase brings expertise in mineral process chemistry, resource finance, and exploration consulting to her role as Founder and CEO of Syntra Refining. Her technical background and industry experience revealed a critical market inefficiency: while mining operations have achieved global scale, refining infrastructure remains fragmented and technologically outdated.

Recognizing this strategic gap, she founded Syntra Refining to develop advanced hydrometallurgical solutions that replace energy-intensive smelting with precision chemical processes, establishing a new standard for both economic efficiency and environmental performance in critical mineral refining.

"Achieving true sustainability in the energy transition demands fundamental innovation in process chemistry. Our approach replaces thermal intensity with chemical precision, delivering superior purity while minimizing environmental impact."

By advancing hydrometallurgical technology and building integrated refining infrastructure, Syntra is establishing the foundation for a sustainable, domestically-secured critical minerals supply chain.
