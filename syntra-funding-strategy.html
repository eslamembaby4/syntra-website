Critical Minerals Refining & Processing — Canada
Syntra Funding Strategy
How Syntra aligns with Canada's $13B+ Critical Minerals Strategy to accelerate domestic refining capacity and build North American supply chain sovereignty
Canada's Critical Minerals Strategy (CCMS) is deploying $13+ billion across 15+ federal departments to accelerate domestic production, processing, and supply chain sovereignty. Syntra's refining platform is purpose-built to access and deploy this capital — spanning the Sovereign Fund, First & Last Mile Fund, Strategic Response Fund, and multiple tax incentives. Syntra operates across all three strategic CCMS themes.
Canada's 3 Critical Minerals Strategy Themes — Syntra's Alignment
THEME 01
Promote Domestic Production & Processing of Critical Minerals in Strategic Areas
Syntra's core mandate: build Canadian refining infrastructure to process domestically-mined minerals into battery-grade, defence-grade, and clean-tech-grade output — eliminating dependency on offshore processing.
THEME 02
Protect Canada's Sovereignty & Economic Resilience by Safeguarding Value Chains
Syntra provides the missing refining link in Canada's critical minerals value chain, ensuring strategic materials stay within allied-nation supply chains and are not exported raw to geopolitically sensitive jurisdictions.
THEME 03
Partner with Indigenous Groups, Domestic Stakeholders & International Allies
Syntra actively structures Indigenous equity participation, seeks partnerships with domestic miners, OEMs, and recyclers, and aligns with IRA and EU Battery Regulation frameworks to serve allied-nation offtake.
